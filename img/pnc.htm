    The PUBG Nations Cup 2022 is the second Nations Cup, also represents the All-Star event in the 2022 Season. Top players are chosen by their peers to represent their nation in competitive PUBG. Only the most talented battle royale players have a chance to take on the world's best for a shot at glory and a share of the prize pool.

    Selection Details[edit]

    Click here for each nation's selection method.

    • Thailand
      • #1 - Popular vote between all players excluding substitutes in the PTS 2022 Phase 1
      • #2 - Popular vote between top selected coaches
      • #3-4 - Mutually decided by the above three players and the team's coach
        • Due to Jacob's withdrawal, the method originally reserved for 4th player will now covered the vacant spot.
      • Coach - Appointed by KRAFTON Thailand
      • Up to two players from the same organization.


    • Vietnam
      • #1-2 - Mutually decided by the coach and PUBG Esports Vietnam.
      • #3 - Voted from a pool of top 5 players in kills from PVS Spring 2022 by pro players and decided by PUBG Esports Vietnam.
      • #4 - Mutually decided by the coach, the above three players and PUBG Esports Vietnam.
      • Coach - Appointed by PUBG Esports Vietnam.
      • Up to two players from the same organization.


    • Indonesia
      • #1 - MVP of the Challenger Rumble Season 3
      • #2 - Decided by the best performed/consistent player both national and international.
      • #3-4 - Fan Voted by the viewers.
      • Coach - Appointed by PUBG Esports Indonesia.
      • Up to two players from the same organization.


    • Australia
      • #1 - MVP of the PCS6 Americas Grand Final decided by an internal calculation taking into account kills, assists, knocks and damage.
      • #2 - MVP of the PCS6 APAC Grand Final decided by an internal calculation taking into account kills, assists, knocks and damage.
      • #3-4 - Popular vote between pro players, voted by pro players.
      • Coach - Mutually decided by the above four players. Additionally players can be appointed as coach instead.


    • Japan
      • #1 - Fan Voted by the viewers, including all Japanese players in the PWS EA Phase 1
      • #2-4 - Popular vote between all players in the PWS EA Phase 1
      • Coach - TBA
      • Up to two players from the same organization.


    • China
      • #1-2 - Reserved for NewHappy as the reigning both World Champion and PCL Champion
      • #3 - Reserved for Four Angry Men as the 3-time PCL Champion
      • #4 - Reserved for Petrichor Road as the reining Continental Champion
      • Coach - Appointed by PUBG Esports CN


    • South Korea
      • All players are selected through the same process
        • Popular vote between all players (30% weight) and coaches/managers (70% weight), 6 players are voted
        • Then, four players are selected by the PNC Committee
          • Includes Producer and Management staffs from AfreecaTV and commentators in the PWS EA Phase 1
      • Coach - Appointed by the Comittee
      • Up to two players from the same organization.


    • Chinese Taipei
      • #1 - Reserved for the best TPE team in PWS EA Phase 1 ( GEX)
      • #2-4 - Fan Voted by the viewers, including all TPE players in the PWS EA Phase 1
      • Coach - Reserved for the best TPE team in PWS EA Phase 1 ( GEX)
      • Up to two players from the same organization.


    • United Kingdom Germany Türkiye Finland
      • #1-2 - Best performed player across three grand finals: G-Loot Spring, PSL Spring Showdown and PCS6 EUW/MEA Playoffs
        decided by best identical placement, with kills as tiebreakers.
      • #3-4 - Popular vote between pro players. Additionally players from the grand finals of ESL Masters Spring and all other PCS6 are eligible.
      • Coach - Mutually decided by the above four players.
      • Up to two players from the same organization.
        • In case of 6 or less than eligible players, The restrictions above are lifted.


    • United States Canada Brazil Argentina
      • #1 - MVP of the PCS6 Americas Grand Final decided by an internal calculation taking into account kills, assists, knocks and damage.
      • #2-4 - Popular vote between pro players, voted by pro players.
      • Coach - Mutually decided by the above four players.
      • Up to two players from the same organization.
